About Me

The story behind every stitch

Welcome to Quilted By Angie

I've been quilting for over 15 years, and every quilt I create is a labor of love. What started as a hobby has blossomed into a passion for creating beautiful, heirloom-quality pieces that bring warmth and joy to homes.

My Process

Each quilt is handmade with meticulous attention to detail. I carefully select premium fabrics, cut precise patterns, and stitch each piece by hand. From design to the final quilting, every step is performed with care and dedication.

Materials

I use only high-quality, 100% cotton fabrics from trusted suppliers. The batting is premium natural fiber, and the thread is top-grade for durability. Every quilt is designed to be cherished for generations.

Custom Orders

Can't find the perfect quilt? I love creating custom pieces! Whether you want specific colors, patterns, or sizes, I'm happy to work with you to bring your vision to life. Contact me to discuss your custom project.
